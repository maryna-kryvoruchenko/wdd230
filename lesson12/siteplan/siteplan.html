Temple Website Site Plan

Site Purpose

The site's purpose is to let the audience explore different temples around the world as well as reserve a hotel close to a certain location.

Target Audience

The target audience is for anyone who has an interest to know or learn more about the temples. That can include members that can enter (12 years or older) or visitors that like to visit temple grounds.
